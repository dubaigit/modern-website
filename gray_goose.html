About Gray Goose

Gray Goose is a premium vodka brand produced in France. Known for its smooth taste and high-quality ingredients, Gray Goose has become synonymous with luxury and sophistication in the world of spirits.

Our Process

Gray Goose vodka is made from the finest French ingredients, including soft winter wheat from Picardy and spring water from Gensac-la-Pallue. The production process involves a unique five-step distillation process, ensuring unparalleled smoothness and character.
